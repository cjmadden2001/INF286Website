About

Highlights

Tiger Woods is an American professional golfer. He played collegiate golf for two years at Stanford before turning pro and making his first start on the PGA Tour in 1996. Tiger is currently tied for first in PGA Tour wins, ranks second in major championship wins, and holds numerous golf records. Tiger is widely regarded as one of the greatest golfers of all time and one of the most famous athletes of all time. He has also been elected to the World Golf Hall of Fame.
